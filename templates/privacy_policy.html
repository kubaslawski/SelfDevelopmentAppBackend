2. Data We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Data: Email address, username, first name, password (encrypted)
  • Authentication Data: Session tokens, authentication information

2.2 Usage Data

  • Tasks & Goals: Task titles, descriptions, due dates, completion status, goals, milestones
  • Activity Data: Task completions, streaks, statistics, progress tracking
  • Group Data: Group memberships, invitations, shared tasks
  • Notifications: Push notification tokens, notification preferences

2.3 Technical Data

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Log Data: IP addresses, access times, error logs (stored temporarily)

2.4 AI-Generated Content

AI-generated content is provided using third-party large language models.

  • LLM Processing: Your tasks and goals may be processed by AI (Google Gemini) to generate personalized plans, motivational quotes, and suggestions
  • Data Sent to AI: Task titles, descriptions, goal information
  • Third-Party Processing: User-provided task and goal data may be transmitted to AI service providers in order to generate personalized content
  • Data Storage & Usage: Depending on the AI provider's policies, submitted data may be temporarily stored or used to improve AI services. We do not have direct control over how third-party AI providers process such data
  • Data Minimization: Where possible, we minimize the amount of data shared and avoid sending directly identifying information

3.1 Primary Purposes

  • ✅ Provide and maintain the Verely service
  • ✅ Manage your account and authentication
  • ✅ Enable task management, goal tracking, and progress monitoring
  • ✅ Send notifications (task reminders, motivational quotes, group invitations)
  • ✅ Generate personalized AI-powered content (plans, suggestions, quotes)
  • ✅ Facilitate group collaboration and sharing

Note: The use of AI-powered features is voluntary and constitutes an integral part of the application's functionality.

3.2 Secondary Purposes

  • 🔧 Debug and fix technical issues
  • 📧 Send important service updates and announcements
  • 🛡️ Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Contract Performance: Processing necessary to provide our services (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR)
  • Consent: Push notifications, AI-generated content (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR)
  • Legitimate Interests: Service improvement, security, fraud prevention (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR)
  • Legal Obligation: Compliance with applicable laws (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR)

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 Third-Party Services

We share your data with the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini (AI): For generating personalized plans and motivational quotes
  • Expo Push Notifications: For sending push notifications to your device

When you join a group or share tasks, the following data is visible to group members:

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your data if required by law, court order, or governmental authority.

5.4 Data Transfers Outside the European Union

Some of the third-party services we use (e.g., AI providers) may process data outside the European Economic Area (EEA).

In such cases, data transfers are carried out in accordance with applicable GDPR regulations, in particular on the basis of Standard Contractual Clauses or other appropriate safeguards provided by law.

6. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Personal data deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Notifications: Deleted after 30 days (sent/cancelled/failed)
  • Logs: Technical logs retained for 90 days maximum
  • Backups: Backup data retained for 30 days, then permanently deleted

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with your local data protection authority

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • 🔐 Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • 🔒 Password hashing (bcrypt/Argon2)
  • 🛡️ Secure authentication with JWT tokens
  • 🔑 Access controls and role-based permissions
  • 🔧 Regular security updates

9. Cookies and Tracking

Our mobile app does not use cookies. We use local storage for authentication tokens and user preferences. Our web API may use session cookies for authentication.

10. Children's Data

The Verely application is not intended for children under 16 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn that a child's personal data has been collected without parental consent, we will take steps to delete it immediately.
